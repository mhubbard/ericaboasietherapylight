Fees & Initial Consultation

I thoroughly enjoy getting to meet new clients and discuss their preliminary thoughts and initial hopes for therapy. I am happy to provide a free phone consultation to introduce ourselves and discuss your individual needs. If interested, this consultation is typically 15 minutes in duration.

All appointments thereafter are 50-minutes in length, provided at the cost mutually agreed upon. I am currently able to offer some sliding scale and student rate appointments.

Coverage: Unfortunately, psychotherapy is not currently covered under OHIP. However, some extended health insurance providers do cover this service. It is always advised that clients check with their insurance providers before engaging in services to determine their coverage eligibility and limits for services sought under a Registered Psychotherapist.
