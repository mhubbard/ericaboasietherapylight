Fees & Initial Consultation

  • Sessions are 50 minutes in length
  • I offer student rates, as well as some sliding scale appointments.
  • Initial 15 minute consult to discuss goals and hopes for therapy, if you choose this – will occur via phone
  • Should you wish to proceed after consult, then we will schedule our first appointment. I will send forms via e-mail for review before this initial appointment
  • Coverage: Unfortunately, most insurance plans do not cover psychotherapy services at this time. It is always advised that clients check with their insurance providers before engaging in services in order to determine their coverage eligibility and limitss
