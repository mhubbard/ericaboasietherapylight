Therapeutic Approach

  • Client and person-centred: you are your own expert
  • Attachment theory better understand our relationship to people/things and our responses to them / life events
  • Acceptance and commitment therapy: suffering is universal
  • Strengths based and resilience – ways of managing in the past help us understand/tackle concerns of the future
  • CBT and mindfulness-based CBT strategies: increase the lens
  • Harm reduction: reduce the risks/harms incurred by actions and choices
